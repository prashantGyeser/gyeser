Why did we create Gyeser

We were tired of trying to order food over the phone. We kept going back to the same place no matter how good or bad that restaurant was, just because that was the only number we had

That is why we created Gyeser. A simple way to find great restaurants and order without having to go through the pain of picking the phone and trying to make the person on the other side of the phone understand what we wanted.
